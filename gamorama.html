Gamorama

Alien Invasion, a game where you dodge lasers and

missiles and try to avoid and get powerups.

Race Dodger, a game where you can dodge things while

having the option of a timer or no timer and if

you are not fast enough, you lose.

The No Jumping Zone, a platformer where you can't jump,

and if you try to, you will be punished.

Exit Path 3, a game where you race to the finish while things try

to stop you, do not ever let them get in your way or else...

Baby Platformer, a game where you try to avoid lava

and spikes and get to the portal. It is easy and it will really

give you some rest for some platformers that are coming soon!
